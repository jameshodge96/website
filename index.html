Knowing Me Knowing You

Researcher and designer based at Open Lab, Newcastle University. My research interests are focused in how we can design research that pays attention to networks of care and aesthetic experience in dementia and currently focused on ways we can design virtual reality to create immersive and shared experiences.

Take a Chance On Me

I graduated in 2017 at Newcastle University with a BSc in Computer Science. During my time in 3rd year, I explored the role of designing tailored virtual reality experiences for people with dementia. As a result, three virtual environments were designed that reflected the participants’ initial experiences. After graduation, I decided to continue the path of academia and I’m currently enrolled in a PhD for Digital Civics at Open Lab. My current research aims to design a peer-to-peer system that is focused on commissioning 360-degree videos as an engagement between communities – of people living with dementia, their carers, and a wider, technologically-linked public.
